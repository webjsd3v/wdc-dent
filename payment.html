Our Clinic has grown to provide a world class facility for the treatment of tooth loss, dental cosmetics and advanced restorative dentistry.

  • We accept most insurances, cash, and checks.
  • We accept Visa, American Express, Discover and MasterCard.
  • We offer financing from City Health and American General. You can get up to 18 months of interest-free financing and we also have longer-term payment plans with a fixed low interest rate available.

Insurance

We accept most insurance plans. Please call our office prior to your first visit to give us your insurance information.

We will gladly help you fill out all those complicated forms or even electronically file them for you. We will work with you to get you the maximum insurance benefit.

New patients: Please bring all of your insurance information with you on your first visit. We will be unable to bill your services to your insurance unless you do. Should you have any change in insurance, please inform the practice before your appointment or any services rendered.
